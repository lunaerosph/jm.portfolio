About

Hi! I’m JM Lagunilla, a graphic artist and front-end developer with a passion for blending creativity and code. I’ve always loved the art of design and the logic of development, so I’m lucky enough to get to do both!

As a graphic artist, I’m all about crafting designs that feel alive—whether that’s through striking visuals, thoughtful layouts, or brand-focused storytelling.
When it comes to front-end development, I enjoy the challenge of turning those designs into responsive, interactive websites that just work—no matter the device.

I believe the best digital experiences are the ones that look great, feel intuitive, and load fast.
My goal is to create clean, user-friendly websites and apps that not only stand out but also deliver seamless performance. Whether you're looking for a fresh website, a creative design, or an interactive app, I’d love to help bring your ideas to life.

Let’s create something awesome together!
